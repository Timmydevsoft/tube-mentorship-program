Introducing The Tube Mentorship Program

The Tube Mentorship Program is a bridge program designed to increase the employability of African University undergraduates and connect experienced professionals with undergraduates seeking career guidance, mentorship and a clear roadmap for career success.

Why Tube mentoring Program?

We are committed to equipping you with the skills and knowledge you need to thrive in today's competitive workforce. As a participant in the Tube Mentorship Program, you will gain the following:

Network Building

Build valuable connections with industry leaders and expand your professional network.

Expert Mentorship

Get paired with a seasoned professional who shares your industry interests and passion for three months.

Real-World Skills

Develop in-demand skills through practical webinar sessions, capstone projects, and one-on-one mentorship guidance.

Confident Boost

Gain the confidence and clarity you need to navigate the job market successfully.

Community support

Join a vibrant community of African students on the same path to achieving career success.

Is Tube Mentorship Program right for You?

This program is designed for driven African University undergraduates who are:

  • Penultimate or final year undergraduate students, in any reputable university polytechnic across Africa.
  • Citizens and residents of an African country.
  • Passionate about being ready for the future of work upon graduation.
  • Willing to dedicate 4 hours per week to the program.
  • Willing to work in a team and collaborate with peers in a group.
  • Have access to the internet and are willing to devote fully to 6 months of intensive training.
